📜 History & Use历史与应用

Astragalus (Huang Qi) has been used in TCM for over 2,000 years, documented in Shen Nong's Classic of Materia Medica (神农本草经). Classified as a superior herb (上品), it may be used long-term without adverse effect. It is one of the most widely used tonifying herbs in Chinese medicine, prized for strengthening the body's defensive Qi (卫气, wèi qí) and supporting overall vitality.

In classical formulas, Huang Qi is frequently combined with Codonopsis (党参) to enhance Qi tonification, and with White Atractylodes (白术) to form the basis of Jade Screen Formula (玉屏风散), a classic preparation for supporting immune defense. Its sweet flavor and warm nature make it well-suited for deficiency patterns involving fatigue, shortness of breath, and spontaneous sweating.

黄芪是中医最常用的补气药之一，被列为《神农本草经》上品，可长期服用而无副作用。历代医家皆推崇黄芪补中益气、固表止汗之功效，其与党参同用可增强补气效果，与白术配伍则构成经典名方玉屏风散的基础，广泛用于体虚易感、自汗短气等症。

🔬 Modern Research现代研究

  • Astragalosides I–IV are the primary bioactive compounds under investigation for their potential structure-function roles in supporting cellular health.
  • Polysaccharides in Astragalus have been studied for their role in supporting normal immune system function as part of a balanced diet.
  • Traditional use in supporting respiratory function and physical endurance is consistent with its classical role as a Wei Qi tonic in TCM practice.
  • 黄芪苷I–IV是目前研究中的主要生物活性化合物，研究其在支持细胞健康方面的潜在功能。
  • 黄芪多糖已被研究用于支持正常免疫系统功能的作用。
  • 传统用于支持呼吸功能和体力耐力，与其在中医实践中作为卫气补益药的经典作用一致。

🧪 Traditional Combinations传统配伍

Combining Astragalus with Codonopsis creates a synergistic Qi tonic effect. Astragalus tonifies Wei Qi and lifts Yang, while Codonopsis nourishes the Middle Jiao and strengthens Spleen Qi. Together they address fatigue, poor appetite, and Qi deficiency with shortness of breath.

黄芪与党参配伍，协同增强补气效果。黄芪固卫气、升阳，党参养中焦、健脾气，二者合用可改善乏力、食欲不振及气虚短气等症状。

This pairing forms the core of Jade Screen Formula (玉屏风散), one of the most celebrated classical formulas for supporting immune defense. Atractylodes strengthens Spleen Qi and dries dampness while Astragalus consolidates the Exterior. Often combined with Siler root (防风, Fáng Fēng) for the complete formula.

此配伍构成玉屏风散的核心，是扶正固表的经典名方。白术健脾燥湿，黄芪固表止汗，常与防风同用构成完整方剂。

A classical Qi and Blood tonic combination. Astragalus provides the Qi foundation (气为血之帅, Qi leads Blood) while Dong Quai nourishes and invigorates Blood. This ratio is the basis for Dang Gui Bu Xue Tang (当归补血汤) — typically 5:1 Huang Qi to Dang Gui.

经典气血双补配伍，体现"气为血之帅"理论。黄芪补气以统血，当归养血和血，二者以5:1比例构成当归补血汤。

📜 History & Use历史与应用

Reishi (Ling Zhi) has been revered in Chinese culture for over 4,000 years as the "Mushroom of Immortality" (仙草, xiāncǎo), documented prominently in Li Shizhen's Compendium of Materia Medica (本草纲目, Běncǎo Gāngmù, 1596). Classified as a superior herb (上品) in Shen Nong's Classic, it is described as tonifying the Heart Qi and Liver Blood while calming the Shen (神), the spirit or mental-emotional aspect of the body.

Historically reserved for emperors and the elite due to its rarity, Reishi was known as "the herb of spiritual potency" (灵). Modern cultivation has made it widely accessible. In TCM, it is particularly indicated for patterns of Heart and Spleen Qi deficiency presenting with insomnia, palpitations, poor memory, and excessive dreaming — hallmarks of Shen disturbance.

灵芝在中国文化中被尊为"仙草"，拥有4000余年的药用历史，在李时珍的《本草纲目》中有详细记载。灵芝被列为上品，具有补心气、养肝血、宁心安神之功效。历史上因其稀少而被视为帝王专用之品，现代栽培技术使其得以广泛应用。中医临床常用于心脾两虚所致失眠、心悸、健忘、多梦等神志不安诸症。

🔬 Modern Research现代研究

  • Triterpenoids (ganoderic acids) and beta-glucan polysaccharides are the primary bioactive compounds in Reishi under ongoing scientific investigation.
  • Beta-glucan polysaccharides from Ganoderma have been studied extensively for their role in supporting healthy immune system modulation as part of a balanced lifestyle.
  • Traditional use in supporting sleep quality, cardiovascular wellness, and adaptation to daily stress is consistent with classical Shen-calming and Qi-tonifying indications.
  • 灵芝三萜（灵芝酸）和β-葡聚糖多糖是正在研究的主要生物活性化合物。
  • 灵芝β-葡聚糖多糖已被广泛研究，用于支持健康的免疫系统调节。
  • 传统用于支持睡眠质量、心血管健康及适应日常压力，与其经典的宁神补气功效一致。

🧪 Traditional Combinations传统配伍

A classical pairing for Heart Shen disturbance. Reishi calms the Shen and tonifies Heart Qi while Ziziphus Seed nourishes Heart Blood and quiets restlessness. Together they address insomnia, anxiety, palpitations, and excessive dreaming from a dual Qi and Blood deficiency pattern.

经典的宁心安神配伍，用于心神不宁。灵芝宁神补心气，酸枣仁养心血除烦，合用可改善失眠、焦虑、心悸及多梦等心血两虚症状。

Longan (Gui Yuan) is a classical Heart and Spleen tonic that nourishes Blood and calms the Shen. Combined with Reishi's Qi-tonifying and Shen-calming properties, this pairing is excellent for forgetfulness, fatigue, and emotional instability arising from Heart Blood and Spleen Qi deficiency.

龙眼肉为经典的养心健脾、补血安神之品，与灵芝配伍，共奏补气血、宁心神之效，适用于心脾两虚所致健忘、疲乏、情志不稳等症。

Astragalus provides robust Spleen and Lung Qi tonification while Reishi adds Shen-calming and multi-organ nourishment. This combination supports both physical energy and mental-emotional equilibrium, making it well-suited for sustained stress, fatigue with anxiety, and immune support in deficiency patterns.

黄芪大补脾肺之气，灵芝宁神兼滋多脏，二者合用气神双补，适用于长期压力、疲劳伴焦虑及气虚体质的免疫支持。
