1. Why TCM Approaches Immunity Differently

1. 为何中医对免疫的理解不同

Western medicine views immunity as a defense system that fights pathogens after they enter the body. Traditional Chinese Medicine takes a fundamentally different approach: instead of fighting disease, TCM focuses on building the body's resistance so pathogens cannot gain a foothold in the first place.

西医将免疫视为病原体进入体内后与之对抗的防御系统。中医则采取了根本不同的方法：中医不是抗击疾病，而是专注于增强身体的抵抗力，使病原体从一开始就无法立足。

☯
TCM Concept: Wei Qi (卫气)中医概念：卫气

Wei Qi — "defensive Qi" — is the body's outermost layer of energy, circulating just beneath the skin and in the muscles. It acts as a protective shield against external pathogens (Wind, Cold, Heat, Dampness). When Wei Qi is strong, you rarely get sick. When it's weak, even minor exposure causes illness.

卫气是身体最外层的能量，循行于皮下和肌肉之间。它充当抵御外邪（风、寒、热、湿）的保护屏障。卫气强壮时，您很少生病；卫气虚弱时，即使是轻微的暴露也会导致疾病。

2. 三大支柱：黄芪、灵芝与冬虫夏草

Of the hundreds of herbs in the TCM pharmacopeia, three stand out as the most clinically effective and well-researched for immune support. Each addresses a different aspect of immune function, and they work synergistically when combined.

在中药药典的数百种草药中，有三种在免疫支持方面最具临床疗效和研究基础。每种草药针对免疫功能的不同方面，联合使用时具有协同作用。

3. Which Constitution Benefits Most?

3. 哪种体质获益最多？

Not every immune herb is right for every person. TCM individualizes treatment based on constitution. Here's a quick guide:

并非每种免疫草药都适合每个人。中医根据体质进行个性化治疗。以下是快速指南：

💡
Quick Constitution Matching Guide体质快速匹配指南

Qi Deficiency (气虚体质): Astragalus is your primary herb. Add Codonopsis and White Atractylodes.
Yin Deficiency (阴虚体质): Avoid hot-nature herbs. Use Cordyceps and American Ginseng instead.
Yang Deficiency (阳虚体质): Warming formulas work best. Combine Astragalus with Cinnamon Bark.
Phlegm-Dampness (痰湿体质): Add herbs to transform phlegm: Pinellia and Poria alongside Astragalus.

气虚体质：黄芪是您的主要草药，可加入党参和白术。
阴虚体质：避免热性草药，改用冬虫夏草和西洋参。
阳虚体质：温阳方剂效果最佳，将黄芪与桂枝结合使用。
痰湿体质：加入化痰草药：半夏和茯苓与黄芪配合使用。

5. When NOT to Take These Herbs

5. 何时不应服用这些草药

Even beneficial herbs have contraindications. Dr. Huang emphasizes these critical cautions:

即使是有益的草药也有禁忌症。黄医生特别强调以下重要注意事项：

⚠️
Important Cautions重要注意事项
  • During acute illness: Do NOT take tonifying herbs while actively fighting a cold or flu — they can trap pathogens inside. Wait until you recover.
  • 急性病期间：在积极对抗感冒或流感时，切勿服用补益草药——它们可能会将病邪困于体内。等待康复后再服用。
  • Before surgery: Discontinue all adaptogenic herbs at least 2 weeks before any surgical procedure.
  • 手术前：在任何手术前至少2周停用所有适应原类草药。
  • Immunosuppressant medications: Herbs that upregulate immunity may interfere with immunosuppressants. Consult your physician.
  • 免疫抑制剂：增强免疫的草药可能与免疫抑制剂发生相互作用。请咨询您的医生。
