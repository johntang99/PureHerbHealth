TCM Lifestyle Guidance

中医生活方式建议
🍲
Diet
饮食
▼
Focus on warm, cooked foods. Avoid cold and raw foods. Recommended: yam (山药), red dates (红枣), ginger (姜), millet (小米), chicken, and root vegetables.
重点食用温热、熟制食物。避免生冷食物。推荐：山药、红枣、生姜、小米、鸡肉和根茎类蔬菜。
🧘
Exercise
运动
▼
Gentle movement is ideal: Tai Chi (太极), Qi Gong (气功), walking, and light yoga. Avoid intense exertion which further depletes Qi.
以温和运动为宜：太极拳、气功、散步和轻柔瑜伽。避免剧烈运动，以防进一步耗损气。
😴
Sleep
睡眠
▼
Prioritize 8+ hours of sleep per night. A 20-minute nap after lunch (午休) can help replenish Qi. Keep a consistent sleep schedule.
保证每晚8小时以上睡眠。午饭后20分钟的午休有助于补充元气。保持规律的作息时间。
🌿
Herbs
草药
▼
Take a regular course of Qi-tonifying herbs for 3–6 months for best results. Key herbs: Astragalus (黄芪), Ginseng (人参), Codonopsis (党参), White Atractylodes (白术).
坚持服用补气草药3至6个月效果最佳。主要草药：黄芪、人参、党参、白术。请遵从医嘱。
